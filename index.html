WHITEPAPER

The Complete Vision

Overview

Captain Guido ($GUIDO) is an ERC-20 token deployed on Ethereum, designed to mobilize capital, community, and culture in support of ocean conservation. The project blends blockchain transparency with structured environmental funding — positioning $GUIDO at the intersection of web3 infrastructure, impact finance, and narrative-driven brand building.

Treasury Mechanism

A 0.1% contribution is applied to all token sales and auto-routed to the $GUIDO treasury. Funds are split monthly: 50% to project operations, 50% donated to verified 501(c)(3) nonprofits focused on ocean conservation. All inflows and outflows are recorded on-chain with monthly transparency reports.

Deflationary Mechanics

$GUIDO incorporates a structured burn framework — reducing circulating supply through unclaimed presale allocations and designated protocol burn events. All burns are executed on-chain and permanent.

Governance

Initially stewarded by the core foundation for compliance and execution efficiency. Governance evolves progressively toward community participation via signalling votes, treasury proposals, and ultimately formal DAO mechanisms.

Transparency Commitment

All treasury wallets are publicly viewable. Monthly operational and donation disclosures published. On-chain verification of all burns and liquidity locks.
