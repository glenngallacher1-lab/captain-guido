THE MISSION

CAPTAIN GUIDO COIN IS A BLOCKCHAIN-POWERED OCEAN CLEANUP INITIATIVE

Join Captain Guido and his crew aboard the Hashwind as they voyage across the globe, fighting pollution and corruption with the power of blockchain technology. Each chapter unlocked means a real donation to ocean cleanup charities at that location.

Armed with the Captain Guido Coin (CGC) and the Nautilus Ledger, we're building a decentralized network of ocean defenders. Every transaction, every token, every mission is verified on-chain—transparent, unstoppable, and making real impact.
